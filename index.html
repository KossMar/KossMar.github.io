Mar Koss

Software Developer

I’m a self-motivated mobile developer, specializing in iOS / Swift. I’m inspired by the multiple languages of the mind that mobile app technology engages, combining my passion for both structure and creativity. I’m enthusiastic about well architectured, tested, quality code and am always looking for new ways to deliver this in my work through new technology and ideas. Attention to detail is important to me. My background in event / project management has sharpened my ability to focus, manage tasks, collaborate with a team and see projects to completion. If I’m not programming, I’m making music, working with electronics or otherwise creating.
